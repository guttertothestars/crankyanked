#Thanks Shimano!

Only cranksets produced between June 1, 2012, up to and including June 30, 2019, need to be inspected.


Dura-Ace and Ultegra cranksets with the following model numbers are affected:

  • Ultegra FC-6800
  • Ultegra FC-R8000
  • Dura-Ace FC-9000
  • Dura-Ace FC-R9100
  • Dura-Ace FC-R9100-P

Shimano says the model number is stamped on the inside of the crank arm near the bottom of the arm.
